What is the difference between the free tier and the membership tier?

The free tier gives you access to all demo courses, which are free forever. The membership tier unlocks unlimited access to all courses, including premium content, along with exclusive perks like member-only webinars and priority support.

Can I switch from the membership tier to a single purchase?

Yes, you can switch to a single purchase plan at any time. However, single purchases only grant lifetime access to the specific courses you choose, whereas the membership tier provides access to all courses.

Do I get a certificate for completing free demo courses?

No, certificates are only available for premium courses accessed through the membership tier or single purchase tier.

What happens to my progress if I cancel my membership?

If you cancel your membership, you’ll lose access to premium courses, but your progress will be saved. You can resume where you left off if you reactivate your membership.

Can I download course materials with the single purchase tier?

Yes, single purchase users can download materials for the courses they own. Membership users can also download materials for courses they access during their subscription period.

Are there any additional fees for certificates?

No, certificates are included with premium courses in both the membership and single purchase tiers. There are no additional charges for earning a certificate.
